Normal Distribution

X ~ N(μ, σ²)  |  f(x) = (1/σ√2π)·exp(−(x−μ)²/2σ²)

Poisson Distribution

X ~ Poisson(λ)  |  P(X=k) = e⁻λ·λᵏ/k!

Geometric Distribution

X ~ Geom(p)  |  P(X=k) = (1−p)^(k−1)·p  (k = 1, 2, 3, ...)

Bayes' Theorem

P(A|B) = P(B|A)·P(A) / P(B)  where  P(B) = P(B|A)·P(A) + P(B|¬A)·P(¬A)
